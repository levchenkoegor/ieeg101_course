# iEEG Online Course

Welcome to the **iEEG Online Course**, an open online textbook on intracranial
electroencephalography (iEEG) recording and analysis. Intracranial recordings
offer direct access to human neural activity at millisecond temporal resolution
and sub-millimetre spatial precision — a combination no non-invasive method can
match.
